About me

Aloha! My name is Megan Kakimoto, and I’m an Hawaiʻi-based copywriter and content editor with a passion for creating original work. I have years of experience developing content and editing written communications pieces for clients in the non-profit, travel/tourism, promotional, retail and healthcare industries. I look forward to the opportunity to support your professional or personal venture through the written word!
